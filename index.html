How Long Should You Rest Between Sets?

This ACE-supported study explores the impact of one-minute and three-minute recovery intervals on the performance of upper- and lower-body exercises (i.e., the chest press and leg press).
The researchers found that three-minute rest intervals are better for female lifters who want to optimize strength and/or muscle building with low-to-moderate intensity resistance training.
